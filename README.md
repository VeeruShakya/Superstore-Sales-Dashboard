## 📌 Executive Summary
For e-commerce and retail brands, holding excess inventory or missing shipping targets directly destroys profit margins. 

This project features an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to give retail executives immediate visibility into supply chain efficiency, customer segmentation, and revenue forecasting. By analyzing large-scale transaction data, this tool allows stakeholders to track day-over-day momentum and predict upcoming sales volume.

## 🎯 The Business Problem
Retail managers often struggle with fragmented data, leading to blind spots in operations:
* Inability to accurately forecast short-term sales, leading to inventory shortages or overstock.
* Lack of visibility into how shipping methods (Standard vs. Same Day) impact the bottom line.
* Difficulty identifying which specific product categories and customer segments are actually driving profitability versus just volume.

## 🛠️ The Analytics Architecture
* **Data Ingestion:** Processed the raw `SuperStore_Sales_Dataset.csv` to ensure accurate formatting for time-series analysis.
* **DAX Engineering:** Built complex Data Analysis Expressions (DAX) to calculate total revenue, profit margins, and average delivery times.
